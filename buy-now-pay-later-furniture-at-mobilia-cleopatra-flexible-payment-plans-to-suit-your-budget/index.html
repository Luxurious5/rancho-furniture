What is Buy Now, Pay Later?

Buy Now, Pay Later is a payment option that allows customers to shop and receive their furniture immediately while deferring payments to a later time. This means you can enjoy your new furniture right away, without having to pay the full amount upfront. It's a flexible way to manage your budget while still securing the pieces you need to create your dream home.
